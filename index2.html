Common questions.

Are your reports accepted by ARAMCO & SADARA without re-validation?

Yes. We hold ARAMCO 9COM accreditation under category 06.04 (Inspection Services) and have completed SADARA vendor qualification. Reports issued under our 17020 scope are accepted directly by both operators.

Can you mobilise across regions for an unplanned shutdown?

Yes. Our three regional hubs share a unified QA platform and personnel competence database. Cross-regional surge has been deployed 11 times in the past 36 months, with average mobilisation lead-times under 96 hours for routine campaigns.

What raw-data formats do you provide for AUT & PAUT?

Native instrument files (Olympus .nde / Eddyfi .ouf / Sonatest .uvi), plus standardised DICONDE archives for long-term retention. Owner audits can be performed in-tool via licensed reviewer software, or via our cloud read-only viewer.

Do you supply Level 3 sign-off, or only field execution?

We provide Level 3 oversight under ASNT-TC-1A and PCN. All procedures are authored or counter-signed by an in-house Level 3, and we can act as the Owner's Level 3 representative under embedded engagements.

How are equipment calibration records traceable?

All instrumentation is calibrated against ISO 17025 traceable standards, with calibration certificates retained in our LIMS for 10 years. Each inspection report carries the asset tag, calibration date, and standard chain.

Have an asset awaiting inspection?

Send your scope & drawing pack. A Level 3 will respond with a method recommendation & price within 1 business day.
